Adopt

Adoptions must be done in person by coming to our shelter. We do not place holds over the phone, email, etc.

Adoption hours:
Monday-Friday: 1pm-5:30pm
Saturday & Sunday: 11am-5:30pm

The adoption fee includes spay or neuter surgery, a microship, a one year Harris County pet license, and age appropriate vaccinations.

Adoptions are on a first come, first serve basis. Animals that are spayed or neutered can leave the shelter that same day. Animals that still need to be spayed or neutered must stay until surgery can be completed. We do not adopt our unaltered animals.
